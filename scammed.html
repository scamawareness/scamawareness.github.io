Been scammed?

Remember to report the incident on the scamwatch website, along with screenshots and other additional information.

This data can then be used to send warnings and follow trends on current scams.
  • First off, cease contact with the scammer.
  • Check bank account for any unauthorized activity.
  • Get the banking company to freeze the account, in order to minimize damage.
  • If you've downloaded anything, then scan your computer for viruses. 
  • File a police report if you've lost money from an unauthorised transaction.
  • If identity theft is suspected, then you can also seek help from iDcare, which is a government funded agency that is able to create the best possible response in your situation.
  • You can claim a certificate to certify that you've been the victim of identity crime, the "Commonwealth Victims' Certificate". This can be provided to the government and other financial companies to secure your credentials and verify your identity.
