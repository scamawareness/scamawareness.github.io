scammed, what now?

(or, you're curious about what scammers can do)

..If the scammer steals your identity, they can:
  • Calling banks, and tricking them into giving access to other accounts, and your money.
  • Opening new accounts and building debt to ruin credit rating.
  • Seizing control of your accounts and changing account information.
  • Claiming government benefits as you.
  • Opening accounts.
  • Using your name as a false identity to perform illegal activities.
  • Misrepresent you, ruin your reputation.

Depending on the scammer, the victim can have repercussions for years to come- financially, and emotionally, identity theft can ruin the victim's life.

If you manage to be extorted or manipulated into giving money to scammers, you will often lose a large sum of money.

Additionally, during romance scams, the victim is often left emotionally distressed, as a result of being betrayed.
